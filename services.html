Tax Consulting and Compliance

We provide comprehensive tax consulting and compliance solutions tailored to meet the needs of businesses and individuals. Our services cover corporation tax, VAT, withholding tax, and payroll compliance, ensuring that all obligations are met in line with Tanzanian tax laws.

Our team of experts assists with tax planning, filing of statutory returns, audits, and advisory on complex tax matters, helping clients minimize risks and optimize tax efficiency. We work closely with you to resolve tax queries, manage correspondence with the Tax Revenue Authority, and keep you updated on regulatory changes.

With our guidance, you can focus on growing your business while we ensure your tax affairs remain accurate, compliant, and strategically aligned with your financial goals.

Payroll and Employee Tax

We provide end-to-end payroll and employee tax services to ensure compliance with Tanzanian tax regulations while reducing the administrative burden on your business. Our services cover payroll preparation, PAYE, SDL, NSSF, WCF, and other statutory deductions, ensuring accuracy and timely submission.

We help businesses implement efficient payroll systems, manage employee records, and respond to payroll-related compliance queries. With our expertise, you can rest assured that all employee tax obligations are met while maintaining transparency and confidentiality.

By outsourcing payroll and tax functions to our team, you free up valuable time and resources, allowing you to focus on your core business objectives while ensuring your workforce is paid accurately and on time.

Accounting and Bookkeeping

Our accounting and bookkeeping services are designed to give businesses accurate, timely, and reliable financial information for effective decision-making. We handle day-to-day bookkeeping, preparation of financial statements, reconciliation of accounts, and development of accounting manuals tailored to your business needs.

By maintaining well-structured records, we ensure compliance with statutory requirements while providing a clear picture of your company’s financial health. Whether you are a small enterprise or a large corporation, our solutions help you streamline operations, improve internal controls, and support sustainable growth.

With our professional support, you can focus on running your business while we take care of the numbers that matter most.

Tax Health Check and Training

Our tax health check services are designed to identify potential risks, uncover hidden liabilities, and highlight opportunities for tax efficiency within your business. We review your company’s tax processes, filings, and compliance practices to ensure full alignment with Tanzanian tax laws and regulations.

In addition, we provide tailored tax training programs to equip your finance and management teams with the knowledge and skills required to navigate complex tax matters. These sessions cover both direct and indirect taxes, compliance procedures, and updates on regulatory changes.

By combining proactive tax reviews with practical training, we help your organization strengthen compliance, minimize risks, and build internal capacity for sustainable growth.
